About This Dictionary

This digital dictionary offers a structured, analytically grounded lexicon for scholars, practitioners, and curious readers engaging with Indonesian gastrodiplomacy. Gastrodiplomacy — the strategic use of culinary culture to advance national identity, foreign policy goals, and cultural outreach — has emerged as a significant dimension of Indonesia's international engagement. This dictionary maps its key concepts, agents, practices, institutions, and food objects, situating each within the broader field of cultural diplomacy and transnational food studies. Entries combine definitional precision with contextual analysis, drawing on cultural studies, political science, food anthropology, and international relations. It is designed as a living reference — expandable, revisable, and open to emerging scholarship.
